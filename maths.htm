About the Department

Welcome to the Department of Mathematics! The Department of Mathematics has highly qualified, dedicated and sincere six faculty members including one TEQIP faculty. Four faculty members are doctorate holders and the other faculty members are pursuing for their Ph.D. The Department of Mathematics offers courses for first and second year UG (B.Tech) students of all branches. The faculties are actively engaged in research activities, whose research interests include Linear Algebra, Operation Research, Fuzzy Set Theory, Wavelet Transform, Functional Analysis etc.
